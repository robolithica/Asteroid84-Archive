WHY THIS ARCHIVE EXISTS

I'm sure you read the disclaimer that we drop on each page, but it IS a bit clinical, so I'll give you my take on this site.

I grew up in the 1980s which, as anyone who was there knows, was a decade more rich in entertainment for kids as there has ever been, and probably ever will be again. As a graphic designer and artist from my wee years, my first love of art was cartoons, action figures, and comic books, and the things that initially drew me to buy any particular one were the logos. Those perfect artistic renderings that supplied me with the answer to every question about what I was about to watch, play with, or read. The logos funneled everything awesome about the IP into a single graphic that pulled you into their story; into their world. Much like the 'friend who introduced you to your future wife', those logos introduced me to characters, stories, and worlds that I would love my entire life. This love became the impetus for this website and the reason I spend hours painstakingly recreating each logo. I wanted to show those wonderful pieces of art the appreciation and love they deserve.
